# News Summary

## Amazon Web Services Outage (BBC News)
Amazon Web Services (AWS) experienced a significant outage caused by a Domain Name System (DNS) error at its largest data center in Northern Virginia. The disruption affected over 1,000 companies and millions of internet users, impacting major platforms like Snapchat, Reddit, and financial institutions. The incident underscores the risks of relying heavily on a few dominant cloud service providers such as AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform.

## White House East Wing Demolition for New Ballroom (CNN)
Demolition has begun on the East Wing of the White House to make way for a new $200 million ballroom planned by President Donald Trump. The project, funded by Trump and private donors, aims to blend with the traditional White House architecture. Trump emphasized the speed of approval, stating there were no zoning restrictions and work could start immediately.
